Kylie Jenner is gorgeous in a white bodysuit and tiara as she promotes her lipstick range

Kylie Jenner was every inch the pop culture princess in a stunning new photo shoot to promote her successful Kylie Lip Kits.


The 20-year-old reality star posed in a white bodysuit and a tiara in two Instagram snaps posted on Friday, as she pretended to talk into a phone shaped like lips.


And as ever, Kylie was her own best advertisement as she smoldered for the camera, wearing a dark pink lipstick shade.

Kylie's sexy lipstick shoot comes after E! shared a sneak peek of Sunday's Life Of Kylie episode, with the star expressing her discomfort at wearing colorful wigs.


Ready to tone her look down, Kylie told hair stylist Tokyo Stylez: 'Honestly, like, guys I don't want to disappoint anybody, but I just want my hair long and black and pretty.'


The star was discussing looks for the 2017 Coachella Music Festival and said: 'I don't want to be a weirdo. I don't want to pull up with purple hair - I'm over it.'


In a confessional, Kylie added: 'I always try to be different. I always try to do new things. I think my following started to get a little bigger when I really was just finding out my style and who I was and dying my hair.'


But she hinted that she may be over that part of her life, adding: 'I'm over keeping up with this lifestyle of crazy hair and wigs and s***. Cool earrings, make-up, and my tits out!'
